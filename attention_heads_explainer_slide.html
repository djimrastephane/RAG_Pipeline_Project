Why your pipeline has 12 attention heads

Because the retrieval encoder checkpoint you load is all-MiniLM-L6-v2, and its config sets num_attention_heads = 12.

12 headsper layer
  • Model hidden size: 384
  • Head size: 384 / 12 = 32 dims per head
  • Layers: 6 transformer layers
  • Total head modules: 6 × 12 = 72

Heads are fixed by model architecture at training time, not by runtime retrieval settings.

What each head does
  • Each head computes a different attention pattern over tokens.
  • Heads run in parallel, then get merged into one representation.
  • Different heads often specialize (local syntax, entities, long-range links, separators, numbers).
  • There is no strict manual label for head 1, head 2, etc.; specialization is learned.
